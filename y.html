About CBTHost.com

Empowering educators and students through innovative assessment technology

Our Mission

At CBTHost, we believe that assessment is a fundamental part of the learning process. Our mission is to provide educators with powerful, easy-to-use tools that help them create meaningful assessments and gain valuable insights into student learning.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of educators and technologists, CBTHost was created to address the challenges teachers face in creating, administering, and grading assessments. What started as a simple quiz tool has evolved into a comprehensive assessment platform serving thousands of educators worldwide.

Our Values

  • Educational Excellence: We're committed to supporting better learning outcomes through effective assessment.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our platform to incorporate the latest educational research and technology.
  • Accessibility: We believe quality assessment tools should be available to all educators, regardless of budget.
  • Privacy & Security: We protect the data of students and educators with industry-leading security practices.

Our Team

Our diverse team includes former teachers, assessment experts, software engineers, and customer success specialists. We're united by a shared passion for education and technology.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Functionality Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These are used to deliver relevant advertisements to you.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a quiz?

To create a quiz, log into your account and click on the "Create Quiz" button. You can then add questions, set time limits, and configure other settings.

How can students join my class?

Students can join your class using a unique class code that you can find in your instructor dashboard. Share this code with your students, and they can enter it when they create their accounts.

Can I export quiz results?

Yes, you can export quiz results in CSV or Excel format. Go to the "Results" section of your dashboard, select the quiz, and click the "Export" button.
